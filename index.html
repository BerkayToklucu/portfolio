About Me

Hello👋

My name is Berkay. I am 17 years old, I am from Turkey and I live in Turkey. I have been working and sharing for 4 years in the field of web design, which I was curious about 5 years ago. I am currently a high school student and I am dealing with web design and coding as a hobby. Among the projects I'm working on right now, the most important project for me is ForzaWEB. In this project, I aim to share different HTML directories, templates and many open source codes that you can use on your web page, and after a while, turn this place into a virtual network environment that more people can use. Although I am not working professionally yet, I am editing my projects to work in the field of web design and coding in the future. If you want to support my projects or ideas, you can reach me from my social media accounts at the top of the page or from my e-mail address below.
